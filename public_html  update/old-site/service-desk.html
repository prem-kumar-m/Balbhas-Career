Service Desk industrialization is observed to be the most compelling IT change. A modern Service Desk is a sine qua non, given the changing business dynamics, digital disruptions, higher degree of social presence, and mounting cost pressure.

Our Managed Service Desk solution, steered by B1-DeskTM model, is designed to enhance the Service Desk of a customer to be a value-added, outcome oriented, automation-based and process centric Service Desk.

The ingredients of our Service Desk solution are:
  • - Service Desk maturity assessment
  • - Service Desk Transformation solution
  • - Continual improvements of Service Desk function

Why Improvements
The improvements are devised to simply the Service Desk processes and embed automation as much as possible, enhance the productivity of Service Desk professionals.

What is unique?
- Industrialized process and tools, leading to only one global/ uniform process/ tool
- Greater scope for achieving synergies across various customers

Committed Outcomes
Enhanced user experience, TCO reduction for entire IT support and higher collaborative service support across Applications, Infrastructure and Business Process Services.

Highly Modernized/ Economical
